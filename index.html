OpenXeroth

An open-source initiative that develops modular, AI-powered pipelines for real-time animal re-identification from continuous video streams.

Starting with livecams like Djuma Cam and extending to drone footage and underwater deployments, OpenXeroth bridges computer vision research and conservation impact by combining cutting-edge detection, tracking, and transformer-based re-ID models in a system designed for edge-to-local-to-cloud execution.

  • Explore on GitHub

What is Hosana?

Hosana is OpenXeroth's first open-source project: a real-time wildlife re-identification pipeline. Built for 24/7 video streams, it leverages state-of-the-art detection and transformer-based re-ID to detect, track, classify and identify individual animals.
